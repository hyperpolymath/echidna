EXHIBIT B — QUANTUM-SAFE PROVENANCE

This software's integrity chain uses quantum-safe cryptographic primitives:

1. SIGNING
   - Primary: Ed448 + Dilithium5 (hybrid classical + post-quantum)
   - Fallback: SPHINCS+ (stateless hash-based, NIST PQC standard)

2. HASHING
   - SHA3-512, SHAKE256, BLAKE3
   - ZONEMD for DNS zone integrity

3. KEY EXCHANGE
   - Primary: Kyber1024 + X25519 (hybrid PQ + classical)
   - Fallback: X25519 (classical only)

4. CONTAINER BUNDLES
   - .ctp bundles signed with Ed25519 (cerro-torre)
   - Attestations: source-signature, sbom-complete

5. PROVENANCE
   - All releases include SBOM (Software Bill of Materials)
   - Transparency log entries for each signed artifact
   - Reproducible builds where toolchain permits
