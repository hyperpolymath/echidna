== Phase 3 — lift everybody to 50 K

At 50 K × 41 provers = 2 M records — 7× the current corpus. Dominant
provers (Lean 131 K, AFP 50 K, Metamath 47 K) are already done. For
others Phase 3 requires:

* Synthetic proof generation (procedural theorems — inequalities,
  polynomial identities) per prover syntax.
* Automated translation — once triangulation ≥ 15 %, use Dedukti /
  OpenTheory to port proven theorems across provers.
* Retire the type-checker-ecosystem shim in favour of real HP-stack
  derivations once our own type-checkers produce them in bulk.
